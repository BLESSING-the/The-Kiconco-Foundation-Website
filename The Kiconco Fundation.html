ABOUT THE KICONCO FOUNDATION

The Kicocnco Foundation is a value-based association that has been formed to see more literate than illiterate Youth in Uganda.
The foundation (being value-based) also has it's objectives which are;

  • To provide scholarships/education support tostudents in vocational training.
  • To mobolize for funds and other resources which will enable the identified youth to access vocational training.
  • To mobolize and sensitize Parents on te need to own up and participate in the education system.
